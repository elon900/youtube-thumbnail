What are the types of youtube thumbnails?

There are 3 types of thumbnails in a youtube video, first in the form of an image, second in the form of images & text & third in the form of text as an image.

  • Image thumbnail- A pure image displayed on the youtube video with no type of text written on it. That is, it is 100% image because the text is not needed at all. The images themselves tell everything about the youtube video. This is considered the best format for thumbnails in terms of visualization. Images thumbnails are usually used for product review videos, vlog videos, and animal videos when the animal does any kind of funny act.
  • Image & text thumbnail- The most commonly used format of thumbnails is images and texts for user interaction. It means this type of thumbnail contains images and text. Images in the thumbnail are represented what type of content is in the video and the text tells what the video is about.
  • Text as Image- This is the worse format of the youtube thumbnail because no visual images are used to make these types of thumbnails. This text format is not like a visualization & is not helpful for youtube video SEO. This type of format is used for a video like, multiple topics, news videos, listing videos, indexing videos, etc. This text image format has a clean background and only text is used to make these thumbnails.
